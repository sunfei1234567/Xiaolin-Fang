About This Project

A short commercial created for Wahaha beverage, focusing on capturing youthful energy and everyday refreshment. The video features dynamic editing, natural lighting, and a vibrant color palette to align with the brand's approachable and lively image. Production involved script writing, location scouting, shooting with DSLR, and post-production editing. The goal was to convey the product's refreshing qualities through relatable scenarios and engaging visual storytelling.
