Featured Projects

Packaging Design

LUNAR NEW YEAR GIFT BOX DESIGN (NEW CHINESE STYLE)

A festive gift box design created for the Year of the Dragon. The overall color palette uses purple to symbolize mystery and auspiciousness, reflecting the traditional blessing "purple air comes from the east." Visual elements include traditional motifs such as the Chinese dragon, fireworks, copper coins, firecrackers, gold ingots, lanterns, and Chinese knots. A gradient color scheme and a new Chinese illustration style were applied to align with contemporary aesthetics while preserving cultural heritage.

Adobe Illustrator | Packaging Design | Illustration | Cultural Elements

Cross-Cultural Communication

CULTURE HARMONY GUIDEBOOK (CROSS-CULTURAL COMMUNICATION)

A guidebook exploring the theme of cultural harmony, designed to promote understanding and dialogue between diverse cultural backgrounds. The content covers topics such as cultural symbols, festival traditions, and communication etiquette across different regions. Visual design incorporates a clean layout with hand-drawn illustrations, infographics, and bilingual text to enhance accessibility. The project aims to present complex cultural concepts in an approachable and visually engaging format, encouraging readers to reflect on inclusivity and mutual respect.

Adobe InDesign | Illustration | Content Strategy | Cross-Cultural Communication
